About Me

 

Anthony Ostuni - B.S. CS & Math @ UMD

I am an undergraduate student in my senior year at the University of Maryland (UMD), where I study computer science and applied mathematics. I am always looking for an opportunity to expand my skills and knowledge of these fields, primarily in those areas with a strong theoretical aspect.

This past summer I was a researcher at the Combinatorics and Algorithms for Real Problems program at UMD. I've previously developed skills as a software engineering intern at Google and the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory.

Research Interests

My primary areas of interest are combinatorics and algorithms, specifically extremal combinatorics and spectral graph theory. I've done some work in those areas (and pursuing more!), as well as work in auction design. See Papers for my publications.
